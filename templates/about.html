Project Story

About Wellora

The Vision

Wellora began as a vision to bridge the gap between modern health science and traditional Indian culinary wisdom. In a world where nutrition advice often feels disconnected from our cultural roots, this project seeks to provide personalized health guidance that honors India's rich food heritage.

What We Do

Using the scientifically validated Mifflin-St Jeor equation, Wellora calculates your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) - the number of calories your body needs to maintain basic physiological functions. We then combine this with AI-powered recommendations to suggest authentic dishes from your chosen Indian state cuisine that align with your dietary preferences and caloric needs.

Key Features:

  • Accurate BMR calculation using proven scientific methods
  • Personalized meal recommendations from 29 Indian states
  • Support for various dietary preferences (vegetarian, non-vegetarian, eggetarian, mixed)
  • Activity level adjustments for total daily energy expenditure
  • AI-powered meal planning with cultural authenticity

The Science Behind BMR

Your Basal Metabolic Rate represents the energy required for vital functions like breathing, circulation, cell production, and brain function. Understanding your BMR is crucial for:

  • Weight Management: Setting realistic calorie goals for healthy weight changes
  • Fitness Planning: Optimizing nutrition for better workout performance
  • Health Awareness: Making informed decisions about daily food intake
  • Meal Planning: Creating balanced meals that match your metabolic needs

About the Creator

Student Project

Computer Science Student

College Engineering Project

This project was created as part of a computer science engineering curriculum, combining passion for health technology with cultural appreciation for Indian cuisine. The goal was to develop a practical application that demonstrates both technical skills and social impact.

As a student exploring the intersection of technology and wellness, this project represents a commitment to creating tools that are both scientifically sound and culturally relevant for Indian users.

Technical Implementation

Wellora is built using modern web technologies:

  • Backend: Flask (Python) for robust server-side processing
  • AI Integration: OpenRouter API for intelligent meal recommendations
  • Frontend: Responsive HTML/CSS with diary-inspired design
  • Data Processing: Scientific calculations for BMR and calorie needs
  • User Experience: Intuitive forms with comprehensive validation

Future Aspirations

This project represents just the beginning of a larger vision to make health technology more accessible and culturally relevant. Future enhancements may include:

  • Expanded recipe database with detailed nutritional information
  • Integration with fitness tracking for more precise recommendations
  • Community features for sharing healthy traditional recipes
  • Support for regional languages and local food variations
  • Mobile application for on-the-go access
